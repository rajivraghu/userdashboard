The Mission: Impossible franchise, inspired by the 1960s television series, follows IMF agent Ethan Hunt (Tom Cruise) on high-stakes espionage missions.
Known for its spectacular stunts, intricate plots, and globe-trotting adventures, the series debuted in 1996 and has released eight films as of 2023.
Themes of trust, betrayal, and teamwork drive each installment as Hunt and his team take on seemingly impossible odds to save the world.
